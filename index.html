// DISEASE INFORMATION

Know Your Conditions

Essential information about the three diseases MediPredictAI monitors — causes, symptoms, risk factors, and prevention.

🩸
Diabetes Mellitus
METABOLIC DISORDER · TYPE 1 & TYPE 2
537M
Worldwide Cases
📖 OVERVIEW
Chronic condition where the body fails to produce or properly use insulin, causing high blood glucose. Unmanaged, it damages nerves, kidneys, eyes, and the heart.
⚠️ SYMPTOMS
  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst & hunger
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow-healing wounds
  • Fatigue & weakness
🔴 RISK FACTORS
  • Obesity (BMI > 30)
  • Family history
  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • Age above 45
  • High HbA1c / glucose
  • Gestational diabetes
✅ PREVENTION
  • Maintain healthy weight
  • Exercise 30 min/day
  • Low-sugar balanced diet
  • Annual glucose test
  • Avoid processed foods
  • Quit smoking
💊 Treatment: Metformin, Insulin, GLP-1 agonists, Lifestyle changes
🩺 Specialist: Dr. Arjun Sharma — Endocrinologist
❤️
Heart Disease
CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDER · CAD · ARRHYTHMIA
#1
Global Killer
📖 OVERVIEW
Conditions affecting the heart's structure and function — including blocked arteries, irregular rhythm, and weakened heart muscle. The leading cause of death globally.
⚠️ SYMPTOMS
  • Chest pain / pressure
  • Shortness of breath
  • Palpitations
  • Swollen legs or ankles
  • Dizziness or fainting
  • Extreme fatigue
🔴 RISK FACTORS
  • High cholesterol (>240)
  • High blood pressure
  • Smoking / alcohol
  • Diabetes
  • Obesity
  • Family history of CAD
✅ PREVENTION
  • Control blood pressure
  • Reduce LDL cholesterol
  • Cardio exercise daily
  • Quit smoking
  • Low-sodium diet
  • Manage stress
💊 Treatment: Statins, Beta-blockers, Aspirin, Angioplasty, Bypass surgery
🩺 Specialist: Dr. Priya Mehta — Cardiologist
🫘
Kidney Stones
RENAL DISORDER · NEPHROLITHIASIS · UTI
1 in 10
People Affected
📖 OVERVIEW
Hard mineral deposits inside the kidneys formed when urine becomes concentrated. Cause severe pain when passing through the urinary tract, and can cause blockages or infections.
⚠️ SYMPTOMS
  • Severe flank / back pain
  • Pain during urination
  • Blood in urine
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Frequent urge to urinate
  • Cloudy / foul urine
🔴 RISK FACTORS
  • Dehydration
  • High uric acid levels
  • Low urine pH (<5.5)
  • High-oxalate diet
  • Obesity / high BMI
  • Family history
✅ PREVENTION
  • Drink 3+ litres water daily
  • Reduce salt intake
  • Limit oxalate-rich foods
  • Avoid excess protein
  • Maintain healthy weight
  • Regular urine tests
💊 Treatment: Lithotripsy, Ureteroscopy, Potassium citrate, Increased fluids
🩺 Specialist: Dr. Ravi Patel — Nephrologist
